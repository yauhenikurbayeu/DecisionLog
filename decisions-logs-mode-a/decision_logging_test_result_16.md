## Memo Draft

# Design-Partner Rollout Plan

This memo confirms the initial release of the AI summary feature to one design partner under a controlled, manually reviewed rollout. The goal is to gather early feedback while maintaining full human oversight of all generated outputs.

**Risks**
- Manual review creates a bottleneck; reviewer bandwidth may constrain feedback velocity.
- Single-partner feedback may not surface edge cases relevant to broader use.
- Dependency on one partner's availability introduces schedule risk if they deprioritize participation.

**Next Steps**
- Confirm design partner onboarding date and assign a dedicated human reviewer.
- Define review criteria and turnaround SLA for each generated summary.
- Schedule a mid-rollout check-in to assess quality and reviewer capacity before any scope expansion.
