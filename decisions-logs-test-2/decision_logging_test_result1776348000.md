## Context

An AI-powered document platform must decide its next-quarter priorities. Four candidate initiatives exist. External and internal pressures include: customers reporting low trust in AI outputs, sales pushing for AI features, engineering overloaded with tech debt, and a competitor that recently launched workflow automation.

## Options Evaluated

- **A) AI contract generation** — New generative AI capability for producing contracts.
- **B) Analytics dashboard** — Visibility and transparency layer for platform activity and AI outputs.
- **C) Workflow automation** — Automation of document workflows and processes.
- **D) Real-time collaboration improvements** — Enhancements to multi-user editing and collaboration.

## Final Decision

- **Primary initiative:** C) Workflow automation
- **Secondary initiative:** B) Analytics dashboard
